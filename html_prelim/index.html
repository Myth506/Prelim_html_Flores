About

"Apex Legends" is a free-to-play, high-octane battle royale game set in the Titanfall universe. Developed by Respawn Entertainment, it features a diverse cast of legends, each with their own unique abilities and playstyles. Players form squads of three and engage in intense, fast-paced combat across a dynamic map filled with weapons, gear, and strategic opportunities. With its focus on teamwork, innovative ping system, and thrilling gunplay, Apex Legends has become a fan-favorite in the battle royale genre.
